Tribute Fun Facts

  • Tribute is the first single of Tenacious D's self-titled debut album
  • The song is a tribute to "the greatest and best song in the world", which they can't remember
  • The song depicts them recalling a time they played this greatest song to defeat a demon
  • Upon defeat, the demon asks if they are angels, but they affirm their humanity
